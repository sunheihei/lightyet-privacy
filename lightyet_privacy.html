LightYet – Privacy Policy

Your health data stays with you.

Last updated: April 22, 2026
LightYet is a personal weight and body-composition tracker. We built it with a simple guiding principle: your health data belongs to you, and only you. This policy explains what information the app collects, where it lives, and how we protect it. Please read it in full — it's short, plain-language, and matters.

🏠1. Who We Are

LightYet is developed and maintained as an independent iOS application. For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at the email address listed in the Contact section below.

📋2. What Data We Collect

LightYet collects only the data you choose to enter. We never collect data passively.

We do not collect: device identifiers, IP addresses, location data, photos, contacts, health kit data, advertising IDs, or any analytics telemetry.

🔍3. How We Use Your Data

All data you enter is used exclusively to power app features you directly interact with:

  • Displaying your weight history, trends, and progress charts
  • Calculating BMI and body fat classification
  • Tracking streak achievements and check-in milestones
  • Personalizing the app interface and widget displays
  • Sending local reminder notifications (if enabled by you)

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the above.

🗄️4. Where Your Data Lives

All users — Local device storage

Weight records and user profile information are stored on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you are a Pro member and have iCloud sync enabled.

Pro members — iCloud sync

Pro members can optionally sync weight records and profile data across their Apple devices via Apple CloudKit — Apple's private cloud infrastructure. Synced data lives in your personal, private iCloud container. Neither we nor any third party can access it. Apple's own privacy policy governs this storage: apple.com/legal/privacy.

Preferences — iCloud Key-Value Store

App settings such as weight unit, theme color, and profile info are synced via Apple's NSUbiquitousKeyValueStore for all users. This is a small, encrypted key-value store in your iCloud account — again under Apple's privacy protections. Notification preferences are deliberately excluded and remain device-local only.

🔗5. Third-Party Services & SDKs

LightYet uses no third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs.

  • No Firebase / Crashlytics
  • No Facebook / Meta SDK
  • No Google Analytics
  • No advertising networks of any kind

The only external system involved is Apple's own CloudKit and iCloud infrastructure, which applies exclusively to Pro users who enable sync, and is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

🚫6. Data Sharing & Sale

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with any third party — ever. We have no backend servers, no databases, and no access to data stored on your device or in your iCloud.

If you contact us via email for support, any information you voluntarily share will be used solely to respond to your inquiry and will not be stored beyond the email thread.

💳7. In-App Purchases

LightYet Pro is available as an in-app purchase processed entirely by Apple's App Store. We never receive, store, or process your payment card information. Apple's payment privacy policy applies: apple.com/legal/privacy.

🔔8. Notifications

LightYet can send optional local reminder notifications to encourage daily weigh-ins. These notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device — no data leaves your device for this feature. You can enable or disable notifications at any time in the app's Settings or in iOS Settings → Notifications.

👧9. Children's Privacy

LightYet is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete such information.

⚙️10. Your Rights & Data Deletion

Because all your data is stored on your own device and/or your own iCloud account, you have full control:

  • Access & export: All data is visible within the app at any time
  • Delete records: You can delete any weight record directly in the app
  • Delete all data: Uninstalling the app removes all local data from your device
  • Delete iCloud data (Pro): Go to iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → LightYet → Delete Data
  • Withdraw preferences sync: Go to iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud and toggle off LightYet

🔒11. Security

Data stored locally on your device is protected by iOS's built-in data protection and your device passcode / biometrics. Data synced via iCloud is encrypted both in transit and at rest by Apple's infrastructure. We follow Apple's recommended security practices for SwiftData and CloudKit.

📝12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify you via an in-app notice. Continued use of LightYet after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
