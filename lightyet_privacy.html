LightYet is a personal weight and body-composition tracker built with one guiding principle: your health data belongs to you alone. We have no backend servers, we collect no analytics, and we cannot access data stored on your device or in your personal iCloud account. This policy explains exactly what data the app handles, where it lives, and how it is protected.

✓ No ads or tracking ✓ No third-party SDKs ✓ Data stays on your device ✓ iCloud is your own account ✓ Apple processes all payments

1 Who We Are

LightYet is an independently developed iOS application. The developer can be reached at the email address in the Contact section. There is no company, organization, or team behind this app beyond a single independent developer.

Because we operate without a backend infrastructure, we have no access to any data you store in the app. All data lives on your own devices and your own iCloud account.

3 How We Use Your Data

All data you enter is used exclusively to power app features you directly interact with:

  • Displaying your weight history, trends, and progress charts
  • Calculating BMI, ideal weight range, and body fat classification
  • Tracking streak achievements, check-in milestones, and weight-loss goals
  • Personalizing the app interface (themes, units, avatars)
  • Populating the home screen and lock screen weight widget
  • Sending local reminder notifications (only if you enable them)
  • Generating shareable achievement cards (processed entirely on-device)

We do not use your data for advertising, behavioral profiling, machine learning training, or any other purpose beyond delivering the features described above.

4 Where Your Data Lives

Layer 1 — Local device storage (all users)

Weight records, body composition entries, and user profiles are stored on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data does not leave your device unless you are a Pro member with iCloud sync enabled.

Layer 2 — iCloud CloudKit (Pro members only)

Pro members can sync weight records across their Apple devices via Apple CloudKit — Apple's private cloud infrastructure. Synced data resides in your personal, private iCloud container. Neither we nor any third party can read or access it. Apple's privacy policy governs this storage: apple.com/legal/privacy. After a reinstall, data automatically restores from iCloud on first launch.

Layer 3 — iCloud Key-Value Store (all users)

App preferences (weight unit, theme, goal weight, profile info) are synced via Apple's NSUbiquitousKeyValueStore for all users — a small, encrypted key-value store in your iCloud account, limited to 1 MB and governed entirely by Apple's privacy practices. Notification preferences are deliberately excluded and remain device-local only.

Layer 4 — App Group shared container (widget)

A small snapshot of your latest weight entry is written to an App Group shared container so the iOS widget can display it without launching the main app. This data never leaves your device and contains only the values needed to render the widget.

7 Data Sharing & Sale

We do not sell, rent, trade, license, or share your personal data with any third party — ever. We have no backend servers, no proprietary databases, and no ability to access data stored on your device or in your personal iCloud account.

If you contact us by email for support, information you voluntarily share will be used solely to respond to your inquiry. It will not be forwarded, stored in a CRM, or used for any other purpose.

⚠️ We may be required to disclose information if compelled by valid legal process. However, because all user data resides solely on your device and in your personal iCloud account, we have no user data to disclose.

8 Home Screen Widget

LightYet includes an iOS home screen and lock screen widget that displays your latest weight and progress. The widget reads a small data snapshot written by the main app to a shared App Group container on your device. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not shared with any process outside the LightYet app family
  • Contains only the minimum values needed to render the widget display
  • Is automatically cleared if you uninstall the app

9 Notifications

LightYet can send local reminder notifications to encourage your daily weigh-in habit. These notifications are:

  • Scheduled and delivered entirely on your device — no internet connection required
  • Never routed through Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) or any external server
  • Stored on-device only; the notification time preference is never synced to iCloud

You can enable, change, or disable notifications at any time in LightYet → Settings → Reminder, or through iOS Settings → Notifications → LightYet.

10 Children's Privacy

LightYet is not directed at or intended for children under 13 years of age (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has entered personal data into LightYet, please delete the app or contact us immediately, and we will provide guidance on removing all local and iCloud data.

11 Your Rights & Data Control

Because all data is stored on your own device and/or your own iCloud account, you have complete control at all times:

  • Access: All data is visible within the app at any time
  • Edit: Update any weight record or profile field directly in the app
  • Delete individual records: Swipe to delete any entry in the history list
  • Delete all local data: Uninstall the app to remove all on-device data
  • Delete iCloud weight data (Pro): iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → LightYet → Delete Data
  • Disable preferences sync (all users): iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → toggle off LightYet
Residents of the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, California (CCPA/CPRA), and other jurisdictions with applicable privacy laws have the right to access, correct, or erase their data. Because we hold no copies of your data, these rights are exercised directly on your device and iCloud account as described above.

12 Security

We implement the following protections for your data:

  • Local data is protected by iOS Data Protection (encrypted when device is locked)
  • iCloud data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest by Apple's infrastructure
  • App Group container data is sandboxed and inaccessible to other apps
  • No credentials, tokens, or sensitive values are hard-coded in the app binary
  • The app requests only the minimum iOS permissions necessary
We follow Apple's recommended security practices for SwiftData, CloudKit, and NSUbiquitousKeyValueStore. The app does not establish any network connections to developer-controlled servers.
